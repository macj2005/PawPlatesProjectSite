About

PawPlates is a free fitness application where you can track workouts, food, sleep, and overall fitness goals to better your health.

We created PawPlates with the user in mind. We offer an easy-to-use, sleek, and modern approach to fitness tracking that puts the focus on you.

TO RUN: Open the zip file, navigate to src/main/java/Main.java and run the file. The app should run and you should be prompted to create an account!
